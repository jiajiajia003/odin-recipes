Instant Noodle Recipe

Description

Instant noodles, also known as instant ramen, are a type of pre-cooked noodle typically sold in individual packets or cups. They are characterized by their quick preparation time, usually just a few minutes of boiling or soaking in hot water. These noodles are often accompanied by flavoring powder and/or seasoning oil.

Ingredient

  • Noodle
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. Put hot water into the bowl with noodle inside
  2. Wait for five minutes
  3. Enjot!
