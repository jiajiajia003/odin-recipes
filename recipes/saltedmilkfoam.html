Salted Milk Foam Recipe

Description

Salted milk foam is a frothy topping characterized by a velvety texture and a unique balance of sweet and salty flavors. It's typically made with a combination of milk (often with cream), a touch of salt, and sometimes sweetener. The result is a light, fluffy topping that can enhance the taste of various beverages like milk teas, coffee, and even fruit drinks.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese 10g
  • Whole Milk 15g
  • Heavy Cream 40g
  • Condensed Milk 10g
  • Salts 1.5g

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese and milk, put it aside to let it cool
  2. Mix heavy ceam, condensed milk and salts
  3. put the mix from first step into the mix from the second step, mix them well and done
